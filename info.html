A Day in the Life

A sample meal plan designed for peak performance and recovery.

Breakfast

Goal: Sustained energy for morning training.

  • Oatmeal with Banana & Berries
  • Scoop of high-quality protein
  • Handful of Walnuts or Chia Seeds

Lunch

Goal: Replenish glycogen and continue muscle repair.

  • Grilled Salmon or Chicken Breast
  • Large portion of Sweet Potato or Brown Rice
  • Large salad with Spinach, Kale, & Bell Peppers

Dinner

Goal: Maximize overnight recovery.

  • Lean Protein (Chicken, Fish, or Legumes)
  • Roasted Brussels Sprouts, Asparagus, & Carrots
  • Glass of Tart Cherry Juice
